How to Play:

Janta: Get a common word, describe it without saying it directly.


Dhongi: Get NO word, figure out the common word, and blend in!


Sahayatri: Get a similar but different word. Try to guide Janta to the Dhongi!

Game Rules

🎯 OBJECTIVE:

  • Janta & Sahayatri: Find and eliminate the Dhongi
  • Dhongi: Survive until final 2-3 players without being detected.
  • Sahayatri: Help Janta identify the Dhongi while disguising your own word.

📝 DISCUSSION RULES:

  • Give clues about your word without saying it directly
  • Listen carefully to spot inconsistencies
  • Ask questions to test other players

🗳️ VOTING:

  • Everyone votes for who they think is suspicious
  • Player with most votes is eliminated
  • In case of tie, no one is eliminated

🏆 WIN CONDITIONS:

  • Dhongi eliminated → Janta/Sahayatri win
  • Dhongi in final 2-3 → Dhongi wins
  • Sahayatri eliminated and guesses Janta's word (and Dhongi is NOT eliminated) → Sahayatri wins
